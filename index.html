HI! I'M GRACE MATSON. Or I'm Fowlren... if you follow me on Instagram. I'm a graphic designer, illustrator, bird enthusiast, music collector, and Zelda fanatic based in Bellingham Washington. When it comes to design, I enjoy branding, motion, and custom typography the most right now. I also do illustration; that's something else I've also enjoyed for a long time.

My image on this page is a linocut print I did a couple of years ago for my premajor portfolio. I feel like it represents me because I really love birds and wildlife.
